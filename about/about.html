The AP test prep platform, designed to help every student succeed.
JoVE24 celebrates diverse learning styles and empowers and supports students in their learning journeys. Our self-paced, asynchronous courses are uniquely positioned to serve individual learning needs and allow each student to curate a learning plan aligned with their goals, schedule and skill level. With our vast library of engaging, animated videos, abundant practice tests and detailed video explanations to test answers – all aligned to AP standards - we provide students with the necessary tools for subject mastery.

Our Legacy

JoVE is the world-leading producer and provider of science videos with the mission to improve scientific research and education. Millions of scientists, educators, and students at thousands of universities, colleges, hospitals, and biopharmaceutical companies worldwide use JoVE for their research, teaching, and learning.

After more than a decade in STEM research, across industries and institutions, JoVE is now foraying into test preparation for high school students with our Advanced Placement test preparation platform – JoVE 24.

Our Content Expertise

JoVE was born from the belief that visualization > reading and visualization through video helps improve transfer of knowledge. With a library of 15000+ videos, all our video content is produced in-house by a talented team of 100s of dedicated video production personnel and our team of subject matter experts from top-tier universities in the US, UK, Germany, Australia and India, with academic degrees in science and education.

Moshe Pritsker

CEO, Co-Founder

Leadership

JoVE was founded in 2006 by Dr. Moshe Pritsker. Born in northwest Russia, he graduated high school at the age of 16 before emigrating to Israel, where he earned his B.Sc. in Chemistry from the Hebrew University of Jerusalem, and his M.Sc. in Chemistry from the Weizmann Institute of Science. Following his studies at the Weizmann Institute of Science, he arrived in the United States where he completed his PhD in Molecular Biology at Princeton University.

Before JoVE, Dr. Pritsker was a postdoctoral researcher at Massachusetts General Hospital, Harvard Medical School. His research works on embryonic stem cells, HIV proteins, and genome analysis were published in prominent scientific journals such as Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences, Journal of Biological Chemistry, Biochemistry, and Genome Research.
